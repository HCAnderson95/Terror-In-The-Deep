# Summary

My Vito Bass Clarinet is very old, and the piece of metal holding the peg in place was worn away. So I decided to make a new one.

# Print Settings

Printer: Da Vinci 1.1 Plus
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: .1mm
Infill: 100%

Notes: 
UNSUCCESSFUL WITH ABS. PETG has worked the best for me

# Post-Printing

You may need to sand the bottom part of the print so it will fit into the Bass Clarinet Peg apparatus.
